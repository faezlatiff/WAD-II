Golden Mix Vol.4 - Singapore’s largest Anikura (アニクラ) event

For those new to the ACG community, Golden Mix is an Anime Music party event, where people gather to rave to their favorite Anisongs and remixes, or simply to chill with friends while enjoying good music. Incase you were wondering, yes we are that Donki song party event!

There will be two areas to the venue. The main central area is where you can dance, rave and enjoy the music best. The surrounding area will be for you guys to chill, away from the speakers, creating a bar-like mood to enjoy the music and still hold a conversation. Come on down and soak in the good vibes!

There is no such thing as being ostracized for not knowing the calls or claps to the songs, just enjoy the music!

Hurry get your tickets before they are sold out again!
